Some games are only used in certain circumstances, and Gamezo.co complies with all the requirements that we could find.

We don't make any changes to the games source code. All attributions, brand names, and allusions to the original webpages are still there. Please get in touch with us straight once and provide the following details if you think that Gamezo.co has violated any of your copyright or intellectual property rights:

  • • The signature—either physical or digital—of the owner of the intellectual property or a designated agent.
  • • A thorough explanation of the violated intellectual property, together with an account of the infringement action.
  • • Where the illegal content is located (for example, the URL on Gamezo.co where it is posted).
  • • A copy of the license allowing you, in the event that you are not the original owner, to safeguard intellectual property.
  • • Specifics regarding the location of the aforementioned item on our website.
  • • A declaration stating that you think the copyright owner, their agent, or the law has approved the disputed use.
  • • Your entire name and contact information.

Kindly forward the aforementioned details to contact@gamzo.co. After reviewing your submission, we'll get right to whatever needs to be done. We will respond to you within seven days.
